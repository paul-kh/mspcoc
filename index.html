Welcome

Are you longing to experience God’s deep grace and love, and its transforming power?

Are you in search of a church family that sincerely cares for you and your walk with God?

Are you looking for trustworthy answers to some of life’s toughest questions?

The Minneapolis St. Paul Church of Christ is a church united by a love for God. We believe deeply in the teachings of Jesus Christ and strive to put them into practice daily. We desire to be led powerfully by the Spirit of God in all aspects of life.

We invite you to join us for worship service on Sunday mornings at 10:00 a.m. or ministry activities happening throughout the week in our small-group ministries. Come discover God’s spiritual direction for your life!

Together, let us draw closer to our God.
